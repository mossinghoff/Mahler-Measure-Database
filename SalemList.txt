Small Salem Numbers

The 72 smallest known Salem numbers. The plastic constant 1.3247... (the
smallest Pisot number) is known to be a two-sided limit point of Salem
numbers, so this list tracks the known Salem numbers below a slightly
smaller value, 49/37 = 1.3243..., which is a continued fraction convergent
for the plastic constant.

For values below 1.3, this list is known to be complete up to degree 44.
There is only one known number in this range with larger degree, 46.

Each Salem number is listed with its degree d and half the coefficients of
its minimal polynomial, since this is reciprocal. Note that the polynomial
listed here may not match the one appearing for the same value in the large
list of known Mahler measures less than 1.3: the minimal polynomial here
may correspond to f(-x) if f(x) is the polynomial in the large list.

Some references appear below. Most of these are sources for searches of
small Salem numbers, or establishing that this list is complete below a
particular bound and degree. The list also includes a monograph and a
survey paper, where many more references for Salem numbers can be found.
